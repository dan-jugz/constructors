Ceramics and Glass

Ceramics and glass have been utilized in manufacturing structual components for a long time since ancient times of the Neolithic period Ceramics tiles are engaged in covering floors, kitchen countertops, as well as fireplaces. Outdoors, ceramics are used for paving staircases, driveways, and terraces. Bathrooms are also furnished with sanitary ware. Glass is a common material in the construction industry and its diverse application include usage in windows, indoor partitions, floors, and skylights. Recently, smart glass has become popular especially in day light control for windows as well as energy-saving panels.
