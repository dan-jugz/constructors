About us.

constructors hub is a goods and services company aimed at providing contractors and any other persons involved in the construction industry with quality materials reliably and at affordable prices. we aim to consolidate all matters construction in one website with professional and accountable services to our clients.
We supply materials, equipment and services in the building and construction industry as well as transport of goods. By this we are the most competitive suppliers of building materials and contractors in Kenya.
By securing our services, you are sure to get quality products at the most competitive rates available with minimum hustles. Our procuring procedures are simple and payment methods convenient including Lipa na Mpesa as well as loan facilities.
The company provides road transport services for; General cargo (mostly Uncontainerized, break bulk, bagged or not bagged), building materials such as ballast, sand, quarry stones, machine cut stones, hardcore, murram, red soil and many others .
