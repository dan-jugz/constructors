Timber Products

Timber continues to be useful for builders and architects. Its versatility has remains relevant due to various reasons that include environmental sourcing & renewability, insulation properties, and tensile strength. Also, timber is used in decorative molding to enable users to enjoy a stylish finishing touch.

Major suppliers of Timber Products in Kenya

Timber Corner

The company has over 40 years experience in the timber and hardware business to accommodate specific timber and hardware preferences. Its product array include boards such as chipboards and plywood, as well as timber products that include cypress, frames, doors, cornices, and veneers.

Shah Timber

The company is a leading manufacturer and supplier of crafted timber products and customized joinery such as kitchens, doors, windows, wardrobes, laminated furniture, hardwood flooring and composite decking. Its pricing is very competitive.

All Africa Timber

The company specializes in the design, manufacture, and installation of high-quality furniture and joinery materials. Its array of products include fitted MDF kitchens and wardrobes. Furthermore, its pricing is quite competitive.

Bhachu Wood

Bhachu WoodProducts specializes in selling a wide array of timber products to create a lasting impression. The list of Products sold under its belt include designer dining sets, wooden floors, kitchen units, wooden interiors, and garden & poolside furniture. Pricing is very competitive.

Timsales Limited

The company remains one of the oldest and leading manufacturer and supplier of timber products in Kenya. Its array of products include fibreboards, plywoods, and flushdoors. Also, its pricing is quite competitive.

Space and Style Ltd

The company offers a wide array of products and services for construction, roofing, plumbing, and decoration purposes. Under its decoration division, it offers floor lamination, fiber cement, roofing systems, and roofcolor bituminous shingles. Its pricing is very competitive.
